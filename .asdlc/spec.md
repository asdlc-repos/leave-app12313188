# Overview

An internal app where WSO2 employees request time off and managers approve or deny those requests.

# Personas

- Employee — requests leave and checks their balance
- Manager — reviews and responds to leave requests from their team

# Features

- An employee submits a leave request by choosing dates and a leave type.
- The request goes to their manager for review.
- Managers see pending requests and can approve or reject them.
- Employees see the status of their requests (pending, approved, or rejected).
- Everyone can check how many leave days they have remaining.
